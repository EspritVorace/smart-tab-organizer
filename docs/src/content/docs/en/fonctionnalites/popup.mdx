The popup opens by clicking the extension icon in the browser toolbar.
It provides a quick overview of grouping and deduplication activity, and gives access to the configuration pages.

## Overview

<ThemeImage lightSrc={popupOverviewLight} darkSrc={popupOverviewDark} alt="SmartTab Organizer popup overview" />

## Content

<ThemeImage lightSrc={popupContentLight} darkSrc={popupContentDark} alt="Popup content with statistics and quick access" />

The popup displays:

- **Statistics** for grouping and deduplication (number of tabs processed)
- **Quick access buttons** to rules, sessions, and settings
- The list of active **pinned profiles**
